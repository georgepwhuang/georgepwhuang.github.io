About me

I am a research assistant at the Computer Science Group of the Centre for Quantum Technologies supervised by Prof. Patrick Rebentrost.

Prior to joining CQT, I graduated from the National University of Singapore with a B.Comp. in Computer Science, where I specialized in algorithms and artificial intelligence. I completed my bachelor's dissertation with Prof. Patrick Rebentrost and Prof. Rahul Jain. I was also fortunate to have worked with Prof. Min-Yen Kan as an undergraduate researcher.

I am broadly interested in algorithms, optimization, machine learning, and learning theory for quantum computation.
