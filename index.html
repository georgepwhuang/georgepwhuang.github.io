About Me

I am a research assistant at the Computer Science Group of the Centre for Quantum Technologies supervised by Prof. Patrick Rebentrost. I go by the name George in daily life.

Prior to joining CQT, I graduated from the National University of Singapore with a B.Comp. in Computer Science, where I specialised in algorithms and artificial intelligence. I completed my bachelor's dissertation under the supervision of Prof. Patrick Rebentrost and Prof. Rahul Jain. I was also fortunate to have worked with Prof. Min-Yen Kan as an undergraduate researcher.

I am broadly interested in algorithms, optimisation, machine learning, and learning theory for quantum computation.

I will be joining the Mathematical Institute of the University of Oxford for doctoral studies under the supervision of Prof. Bálint Koczor and Prof. Simon Benjamin starting October 2024.
