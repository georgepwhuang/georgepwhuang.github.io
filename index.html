About me

I am Po-Wei (George) Huang, a research assistant specializing in quantum algorithms at the Centre for Quantum Technologies. I graduated from National University of Singpore with a B.Comp. in Computer Science, where I specialized in algorithms and machine learning. My current PI is Prof. Patrick Rebentrost, who also supervised my bachelor's thesis on post-variational quantum neural networks with Prof. Rahul Jain.

My current research interests include:

  • hybrid quantum-classical algorithms
  • near-term quantum computation
  • quantum machine learning
  • classical shadow tomography

I've previously worked with Prof. Min-Yen Kan at the Web Information Retrieval / Natural Language Processing Group (WING) on supervised and semi-supervised strategies for contextual document structure extraction with medium-sized transformer language models.
